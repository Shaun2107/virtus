About Us

Who Are We?

We are a team of change makers hoping to make our mark through innovative builds coupled with determination to build the world's tomorrow with the dreams of today.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower people of determination and provide them with the resources they need to make transportation problems an issue of the past! Along with easing the lives of people of determination Virtus will be pivotal in maintaining the innovative stride among youngsters. Our website has enabled the differently-abled to connect with each other, feel a new sense of independence, and will ultimately lift their spirits. With this, we are able to contribute and make a change in society and further harbor the theme of innovation within society.
