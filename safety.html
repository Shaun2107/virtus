Our Commitment To Safety

Your safety will always remain our main priority.

To ensure the utmost safety of all our customers we have put into place a stellar safety precaution system.
  • All drivers are vaccinated and mandated to keep their masks on at all times.
  • Customers can be tracked by their loved ones Incase of any issues that come about with the said trip.
  • All drivers are trained to provide first aid in order to address any issues.
  • All vehicles are cleaned and sanitized on a regular basis.
  • Customers are required to wear a mask at all times during the ride.
